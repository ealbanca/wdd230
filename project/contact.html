Cost: Free

Benefits:

- Networking with local businesses
- Listing on the Chamber website

Bronze Membership

Cost: $200 per year

Benefits:
- Chamber directory listing
- Invitation to general networking events
- Discounted rates on events and programs

Silver Membership

Cost: $500 per year

Benefits:
- All Bronze level benefits
- Spotlight position on the Chamber homepage
- Invitations to special events, webinars, and training sessions
- Access to advanced business development resources
- Discounted advertising opportunities

Gold Membership

Cost: $1,000 per year

Benefits:
- All Silver level benefits
- Featured in the Chamber monthly newsletter
- VIP invitations to exclusive events
- Free or highly discounted event sponsorships
- Complimentary training and business consulting sessions

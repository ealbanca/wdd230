Scoots Motor Scooter Rental Agreement

This Rental Agreement is entered into by and between Scoots (the "Company"), a motor scooter rental service located on the island of Cozumel, Mexico, and the undersigned customer (the "Renter"). By submiting this form, the Renter agrees to abide by all the terms and conditions set forth below.

Insurance and Liability

Insurance Coverage: The Company offers basic insurance for the rental period. Optional full coverage can be purchased at an additional cost. The Renter is responsible for any damages, theft, or loss not covered by the insurance. - Basic Insurance Coverage: Covers third-party liability (damages to others). - Full Coverage (Optional): Covers damage to the rental vehicle, theft, and personal accident insurance.

Renter's Responsibility

The Renter agrees to: - Operate the vehicle in a safe and lawful manner. - Ensure the vehicle is securely parked when not in use. - Be responsible for any damage, loss, or theft not covered by insurance. - Follow local traffic laws and regulations. - Wear a helmet and other safety gear (if applicable).

Rental Conditions

Age Requirement: Renters must be at least 18 years old to rent a motor scooter, and at least 21 years old for ATVs and jeeps. License Requirement: A valid driver’s license must be presented at the time of rental. International drivers may use a valid driver’s license from their home country or an International Driver’s Permit. Safety: - Helmets are required for motor scooter rentals. - Renter agrees to follow all traffic regulations and road signs. - Alcohol and drug use are prohibited while operating the rental vehicle.

Cancellation and Refunds

Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made more than 48 hours prior to the rental start time will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the rental start time will incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the total rental cost. No-Show Policy: If the Renter fails to show up for the scheduled rental, no refund will be issued.

Return of Vehicle

The Renter agrees to return the vehicle to the designated location by the agreed time and date. A late fee will apply for vehicles returned after the scheduled return time, and the Company reserves the right to charge additional fees for excessive mileage.
